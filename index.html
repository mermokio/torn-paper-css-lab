Recipe notes

The CSS moves that make the paper feel physical.

  1. Layer repeating gradients with slightly different scales to fake fiber direction.
  2. Break clean edges with masks, clipped pseudo-elements, and offset shadow bands.
  3. Use barely-there color shifts instead of loud contrast so the paper keeps its softness.
  4. Stack two or three sheets with rotation changes to make the tear feel tactile.
